HighScope

What is the HighScope educational model?

The HighScope educational model emphasizes “individual active learning”. Active learning means that students have first-hand experiences with people, things, events, and ideas. Children's interests and choices are at the heart of the HighScope program.

Children build their own knowledge through interactions with the world and people around them. Children take the first step in the learning process through making choices and implementing their plans and decisions. Teachers, caregivers, and parents provide children with emotional, material, and intellectual support. In active learning, adults open up children's minds through diverse interactions of materials and care. Through “racks,” adults help children gain knowledge and develop creative problem-solving skills.

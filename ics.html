Vision and Direction

ICS's Program

The ICS Primary Program is a combination of the national standard curriculum and the Design Thinking method. With this method, children's learning process goes through 4 steps of Feel - Imagine - Do - Share (referred to as FIDS), helping children gain real knowledge and real capacity to solve real problems, not just stop. back in books.

School counseling

A happy learning environment, preventing violence – school bullying

ICS is a friendly, close-knit environment that respects differences and connects individuals. Schools actively prevent school bullying, encouraging appropriate behaviors by building social skills and values ​​in students. Teachers are selected with the criteria of controlling their own emotions and are periodically trained and trained in expertise, skills, and practices to keep and stabilize their emotions, to become effective teachers with the application of active methods in teaching students.

Register to go to school

ICS aims to provide a positive experience and smoothest admissions process for families looking to join the school community. The school accepts applications for admission from students of all nationalities throughout the school year so that they can participate in the international environment and grow from the school's educational program.

The school strongly encourages families to apply for admission as soon as possible to avoid being placed on a waiting list.

Parents can choose to register via email, register online or in person at the School's Admissions Office at : 92 Nguyen Huu Canh, Ward 22, Binh Thanh District, HCMC.

Parents and students will go through three basic stages to complete the enrollment process at the ICS Preschool - Primary School System as follows:

Step 1: Register for admission

Complete the enrollment application form (online or in person). Parents can download the form here

Pay the enrollment or reservation fee (if applicable) for the Elementary Level (this fee will be deducted from the tuition fee).

Step 2: Check input

All students are required to participate in an interview, application review and entrance assessment at ICS.

Step 3: Admission

In case of admission, the school will notify parents via email and phone about the admission procedure within 05 to 10 working days from the entrance assessment, parents need to pay the fees and submit the relevant documents within 5 days from the date of receiving the Letter of Admission.

Welcome to Kindy City

Foreword

Welcoming parents and congratulating your children on becoming citizens of KINDY CITY International Kindergarten - where they will continuously discover, experience and develop perfectly along the way. early awakening.

Everyone knows that preschool is the first level of learning, which has the foundation to help children walk steadily in the next and lifelong learning stages. The outstanding feature at this stage is that children often learn through play, and play acts as a connection, fostering their cognitive ability and understanding. In this context, the family – where children often begin the process of enlightenment, and the school – including the teachers and related service personnel, must work together and synergize for a goal. in general, that is, for the GROWTH, COMPREHENSIVE DEVELOPMENT, MAXIMUM OF EVERY CHILD, according to the motto: YOUR CHILDREN, OUR SURGICAL OBJECTS.





Infrastructure

Different from the traditional way of learning, the classroom at KINDY CITY is not framed in the classroom space, but also expands to the outdoors, outside the school. In each classroom, there are fully equipped teaching aids such as computers, LCD projectors, safe, airy and clean sleeping and resting facilities. The learning space also extends to Music and Drama function rooms – Documentation Room, where students can use equipment for skill training such as personal computers, Ipad tablets and SmartBoard smart boards. etc…

In addition, the school provides learning software installed on IPAD for children as well as a list of supplementary books so that parents can strengthen their knowledge at home with their children.

Health care

The motto "Children come first" is reflected in the compliance with management, supervision and control procedures for raising and caring activities, and risk control procedures prescribed by management levels. Child care is strictly regulated, ensuring maximum safety and always taking care of the health of all children – from shift assignment, to handover in supervision, are guaranteed by teachers and students. sample and management levels seriously implemented.

At KINDY CITY, children receive periodic health checks and oral health care from prestigious international health partners in the city. Children are regularly monitored for growth in height, weight and health weekly, thereby creating an individual medical record for each child.

School meals are provided by THE CATERERS School Food Company - a reputable meal supplier, currently providing meals to most international schools in Ho Chi Minh City. Ho Chi Minh. The students' diet and nutrition are made strictly according to the requirements of the Health and Food Hygiene and Safety of the District and the City, and are also subject to the supervision and inspection of these agencies. The school's teachers guide and observe the students during mealtime, especially not only to guide the correct and polite eating style, but also to help them understand the basics of nutrition. acquire healthy living habits, eat healthy and develop balance from an early age.

Leadership team

TEAM TEACHING: Domestic and foreign teachers teach together on an agreed lesson topic in order to exploit, consolidate and expand knowledge and improve skills for students. At the same time, they gain many perspectives and boldly express their personal opinions and feelings. The school makes full use of advanced pedagogical methods to develop multi-faceted children, following a relaxing, gentle but effective learning method "LEARN THROUGH PLAY" - combining lessons with games inside and outside the classroom. and arouse in the children the interest and eagerness to learn.

Core values

To provide a safe, friendly and effective environment for children's learning and personality development, teachers, service personnel and students throughout the school are committed to becoming active and responsible actors. responsibility, always demonstrate and uphold the following values ​​and standards:
• Honesty: doing the right thing and having an attitude of respect for what you do.
• Respect: respect and uphold the dignity of oneself, others and the environment.
• Empathy: seek to understand and appreciate the feelings and behaviors of others.
• Cooperation: making efforts together, acting for the most common purpose and goals.
• Optimism: always have a positive attitude and hope for a better future.

VAS (Vietnam Australia International School System) Located in the City Center. Ho Chi Minh City

Welcome to VAS

Vietnam Australia International School System (VAS) was established in 2004, training from preschool to high school. VAS is licensed by the Vietnamese government to operate and is part of the national education system. Currently, VAS is teaching more than 7,000 students from kindergarten to grade 12 at 7 campuses located in Ho Chi Minh City.

At VAS, we understand that nothing is more important to children than the opportunity to receive the best education that will help them succeed in the future. We are proud that VAS is the place that gives students these backgrounds. We apply the most up-to-date teaching methods to equip students with a strong cultural background and academic knowledge, along with practical life skills and personal values ​​and qualities. good. We also aim to create a comfortable, healthy, and safe school environment, where every student has an enjoyable experience and an opportunity to demonstrate kindness, love, and respect. where they are actively encouraged to maximize their natural potential. The VAS Core Values ​​Program will support this goal and is outlined in this document.

We also want each student to achieve good academic results and grow intellectually and socially. We believe that today's young talents will reap great success in the future when they are equipped with a good education from an early age. We hope that your time at VAS will be the most beautiful and memorable time in your student's life.



Vision and Direction

International Certification

Member of Cambridge International Schools VAS is accredited as a Member of Cambridge International Schools (Cambridge School) by the Cambridge Assessment Authority for International Education (CAIE). CAIE is a leading educational institution offering a wide range of international diploma and degree programs to students aged 5 to 19 years. Students at VAS will sit for Cambridge international qualifications such as Cambridge Checkpoints, IGCSE and A Levels.

VAS's Vision

To be an excellent school system that equips students with a strong intellectual foundation through a harmonious combination of the National Curriculum and the Cambridge International Academic Program to help them succeed. in life, while preserving the good values ​​of the nation.

Mission

VAS will clearly define core values ​​and best enable all stakeholders to dedicate themselves to achieving the highest standard of each value in the spirit of continuous improvement.

VAS will harmoniously teach the National Education Program and the International Education Program, helping students become fluent in both Vietnamese and English.

VAS will build an excellent team of national and international managers, teachers and staff who enthusiastically and effectively implement VAS' educational programs.

VAS will continuously build regular communication and close relationships with parents in which parents play an effective support role, helping teachers develop to the fullest potential of students.

VAS will attract more and more students as well as expand the number of educational institutions on the basis of responsibility and sustainable development.

Modern equipment

Classrooms and departments of Music, Painting, Studio, Gymnastics, and laboratories are designed in accordance with age psychology and using high-tech equipment. Students can use audio-visual room, computer room with high-speed Internet connection.

A modern library system with a variety of reference books corresponding to each subject, meeting the reference needs of both teachers and students. In particular, the English materials of the Cambridge International Education Program as well as the general library management system are provided by Follett, a leading organization in providing materials and managing libraries in the world.

Vietnam Australia International School System is one of the pioneer schools in bringing high standards of facilities to bring students comfort in learning, playing and experimenting.

Building standards

VAS' educational institutions are designed and built according to strict domestic and international standards, ensuring maximum regulations on safety, hygiene, fire prevention, ventilation, and contributing to safety. creating a learning environment that is both comfortable and safe for students.

Sanitize according to international standards

Classrooms, cafeterias, playgrounds, swimming pools, toilets are always kept clean and disinfected according to international standards to ensure a clean and safe learning, living and playing environment for all students.

Convenient transportation

The location in the city center districts brings convenience for parents to pick up and drop off their children. Or parents can safely send their children under the school's professional shuttle system.

Parents and students will go through three basic stages to complete the enrollment process at the VAS Preschool - Primary School System as follows:

Step 1: Register for admission

Complete the enrollment application form (online or in person). Parents can download the form here

Pay the enrollment or reservation fee (if applicable) for the Elementary Level (this fee will be deducted from the tuition fee).

Step 2: Check input

All students are required to participate in an interview, application review and entrance assessment at VAS.

Step 3: Admission

In case of admission, the school will notify parents via email and phone about the admission procedure within 05 to 10 working days from the entrance assessment, parents need to pay the fees and submit the relevant documents within 5 days from the date of receiving the Letter of Admission.

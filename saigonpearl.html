Welcome to ISSP

Saigon Pearl International School (ISSP) is a monolingual international school for children aged 18 months to 11 years old in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam.

Our teaching and learning methods are based on the International Baccalaureate (IB) and American standards. This approach supports the holistic development of students and provides them with a balanced education.

We are proud to be an international school that offers outstanding learning opportunities for students, including Art, Music, Indigenous Languages, Physical Education classes and an innovation hub (Innovation Hub). ) including STEAM, Studio and Robotics.

International curriculum, dynamic teaching and learning methods help students of Binh Thanh District International School ISSP have the opportunity to transfer to prestigious high schools in Ho Chi Minh City such as Ho Chi Minh City International School Ho Chi Minh City (ISHCMC) or American Academy ISHCMC-AA or anywhere in the world.

As an International Baccalaureate (IB) Primary School (PYP) compliant school with ISSP Values, the school is guided by the ten IB Learner Profiles (IB Learner Profile).

Student Qualities IB embodies a range of human competencies and responsibilities including intellectual, personal, as well as emotional and social development. ISSP, as an International Baccalaureate Primary School (PYP) candidate school, understands that developing and demonstrating IB Student Qualities will provide our community with an important foundation international spirit.

School Values

ISSP values are Honesty, Respect, Responsibility, Care and Understanding. These values are consistent with the ten typical characteristics of students in the IB program (IB Student Qualities), whose important mission is to develop all-round students the skills and qualities to contribute to their success. future development of society.

Integrity: ISSP students are expected to be honest and develop integrity throughout their time at school. Through a safe and nurturing environment, students can know that they will be supported throughout their education through the 'Recovery Justice' approach.

Caring : Showing care and compassion is a fundamental aspect of ISSP expectations. Students must demonstrate care and compassion through all interactions with their peers, with the community, and with their surroundings.

espect: Students develop respect through cultivating their own integrity and responsibility, accepting and appreciating differences. Students develop in their respect for themselves and for each other, the community and the environment.

Accountability: Students develop a sense of responsibility through the pursuit of personal interests and passions, commitment to learning, personal achievement, perseverance, and resilience. At ISSP, we encourage a passion for learning and an inquisitive spirit, and celebrate success and achievement in all of our students' endeavours, and throughout their time at school. develop the skills to take increasing responsibility for their own learning and behaviour.

Intelligence: At ISSP, we value confidence, creativity and innovation, leadership and initiative, as well as a balanced and holistic education. Opportunities are created for all members of the community to exercise initiative and leadership and, in the process, develop even greater wisdom. We aim to motivate our students in all aspects of learning and encourage progress as they go from knowledge, to understanding, and then to wisdom.

Register to go to school

ISSP aims to provide a positive experience and smoothest admissions process for families looking to join the school community. The school accepts applications for admission from students of all nationalities throughout the school year so that they can participate in the international environment and grow from the school's educational program.

The school strongly encourages families to apply for admission as soon as possible to avoid being placed on a waiting list.

Parents can choose to register via email, register online or in person at the School's Admissions Office at : 92 Nguyen Huu Canh, Ward 22, Binh Thanh District, HCMC.

Admission process of Saigon Pearl International School:

STEP 1

Parents will first need to complete the Online Application Form and send all required documents via email: admissions@issp.edu.vn. All required forms, fees and paperwork must be submitted before an application can be considered for admission. Applications will be reviewed in chronological order. Please see the list of required documents below the page.

STEP 2

Once the application is received and the registration fee is received from the parent, the Admissions Office will review the application. The school may ask for more information about your child from your child's family or previous school. The school will then contact the family to schedule an age assessment with the School Board, followed by an interview. Next, the family will be notified of whether the child meets the criteria for admission.

STEP 3

Once your child has been admitted to school, parents need to complete the Medical Examination Form and return it to the school prior to the child's start date.
